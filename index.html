My thoughts

Nowadays I like things that are simple, minimalistic and straight forward. It saves lot of our time and gives us a distraction free environment. When the time goes, it will create a feeling of flatness and boring. It make us to move away from it and choose a different path. But after experiencing many paths and changes, ultimately it makes us to come back again with more awareness and then appreciate how much it has really helped everyone multi-dimensionally and unconditionally. That is the beauty of it. If you have noticed, this website is also trying to reflect that in a way. This doesn't mean that other paths are bad. Each serves its own purpose and we are independent to choose anything according to the needs. If you don't like being too simple, that is completely fine. I am just trying to be simple. I might not be simple as you think. Everything is just a process and its by-product!

C is also a simple, minimalistic and straight forward programming language with very minimal abstraction. Easy to remember all syntax. But it is not easy to create all kinds of applications. If you aren't skilled enough in C programming, then any kind of vulnerabilities are possible in your program. We have to manually manage resources most of the time. But still I choose this language because of two reasons. First, due to its simplicity, minimalistic syntax. Second, it shows how programming actually works. There are very minimal abstraction. I am a person who likes to understand the fundamentals and likes to know atleast one level deeper. Thats it! One of the efficient things to do in C programming is to create libraries and then reuse it. All the said regarding C is suitable for personal projects.
